Welcome to International University

International University (IU) is one of eight member universities under Vietnam National University Ho Chi Minh City, established in December 2003.

This is the first and only multidisciplinary public university in Vietnam that uses English entirely in training, teaching, and research.

“The goal of International University is to become one of the leading research universities in Vietnam and the region.”

Overview of IU

Studying Environment

Communication between lecturers and students is conducted almost entirely in English.

Modern Facilities

IU offers modern classrooms, laboratories, and one of the most vibrant canteens among all campuses.

Professional Development

IU provides opportunities for students to enhance their skills through international programs, workshops, and hands-on projects.
